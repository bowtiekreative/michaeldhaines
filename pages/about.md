## Biography

For over two decades, Michael D. Haines has been delivering talks to private enterprise and students ranging from elementary to college levels.

### Personal Challenge

Michael was born with Cerebral Palsy (C.P.), a condition that impairs motor skills — but it has never stopped him. He uses an electric wheelchair and assistive speech technology.

### Expertise

Michael is a champion for excellence in customer service. He uses wit, humor, and a special insight born from his lived experience to help businesses understand how to serve customers with disabilities/challenges.
